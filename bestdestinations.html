Gran Canaria

Gran Canaria is the third largest island of the Canary Islands. It is located in the Atlantic coast of Northwest Africa. Canary Islands is part of Spain and has a perfect climate, as it is springtime almost all the year. This island has also a great diversity. You can go to the beach, to the mountain, to a volcano, to sand dunes, phishing ... Las Palmas de Gran Canaria is the capital of Gran Canaria. The average price for two people that travel to Gran Canaria for one week is about 1400€

Venice

Venice is a city in Italy. It was once the centre of a maritime republic. During late medieval Europe, it was the greatest seaport and the continent's commercial and cultural link to Asia. Venice is one of the world's oldest tourist and cultural centres. Venice is famous for all the amount of canals it has all over the city. In Venice people don't travel by car, as there are no roads. Instead they travel by small boats through the canals. A one-week trip to Venice for one person costs around 1600€

Mexico City

Mexico City is the capital of Mexico and it is located in the Valley of Mexico. It was founded by spanish conquerors in 1521. It is also one of the most important cultural and financial centers in the world. Mexico City has a subtropical highland climate. The average annual temperature varies from 12 to 16 ºC. The temperature is rarely below 3 ºC or above 30 ºC. This city is one of the top ten cities in the world with highest number of museums. It has about 170 museums. The price for one person that travels to Mexico City for 7 days is 900€

Paris

Paris is the capital of France. It is also one of the most popular cities in this country. It has a population of about 2 million citizens and it is located in the nothern part of France. It is known as one of the most beautiful cities in the world. In Paris you can find lots of historical buildings and monuments such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre Dame Cathedral, Bastille, Louvre, ... Paris has a semi-continental climate, during the summer is hot, but temperatures do not exceed 30 degrees, whereas winters are too cold, reaching 5 degrees. One person that travels to Paris for seven days spends around 1300€

Rome

Rome is a historical city and the capital of Italy. It is also the third most visited city in Europe and the 14th worldwide. Rome has a mediterranean climate, with mild winters and very hot summers. It is located in the middle of Italy between Florence and Naples. This city was once the center of a powerful Roman state. Rome has a lot of history, with many ancient monuments such as the Roman Colosseum, the Forum, the Palatine Hill, ... The average price of a one-week trip to Rome for one person is around 1000€

Munich

Munich is the capital of Bavaria, located in the south of Germany. It is Bavaria's largest city and the third largest city in Germany. Munich is connected by rail to all the main cities of Germany and Austria. It has a continental climate, which means that it has cold winters and warm summers. Munich has a huge number of museums as well as amazing parks. Englischer Garten is the most popular one. A 3-days trip to Munich for one person costs about 800€

Gran Canaria

Gran Canaria is the third largest island of the Canary Islands. It is located in the Atlantic coast of Northwest Africa. Canary Islands is part of Spain and has a perfect climate, as it is springtime almost all the year. This island has also a great diversity. You can go to the beach, to the mountain, to a volcano, to sand dunes, phishing ... Las Palmas de Gran Canaria is the capital of Gran Canaria.

Venice

Venice is a city in Italy. It was once the centre of a maritime republic. During late medieval Europe, it was the greatest seaport and the continent's commercial and cultural link to Asia. Venice is one of the world's oldest tourist and cultural centres. Venice is famous for all the amount of canals it has all over the city. In Venice people don't travel by car, as there are no roads. Instead they travel by small boats through the canals.

Mexico City

Mexico City is the capital of Mexico and it is located in the Valley of Mexico. It was founded by spanish conquerors in 1521. It is also one of the most important cultural and financial centers in the world. Mexico City has a subtropical highland climate. The average annual temperature varies from 12 to 16 ºC. The temperature is rarely below 3 ºC or above 30 ºC. This city is one of the top ten cities in the world with highest number of museums. It has about 170 museums.
